Instructions

1. If you are a customer, visit the menu page and add your food to cart.

2. Once you have added your food to cart you should fill the cart form.

3. Fill the details required, and click submit.

4. You can see your order status in the Orders page.

5. That's it! Now pay the delivery person by cash/QR, when he/she reaches (Don't hesistate to tip them).

6. If you are an owner of a canteen, or a chef, do visit the 'Cooks page' to proceed with making changes in the menu.

About Website

Welcome to IIITeatz Food Delivery, your go-to source for delicious and convenient meals delivered right to your doorstep. At IIITeatz, we are passionate about food and believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, tasty meals, no matter how busy their lives may be.

Our mission is to make it easy for you to enjoy canteen-quality food at home, at work, or on the go. We partner with top-rated local canteens to bring you a wide selection of cuisines and dishes, from classic comfort food to exotic international fare. And with our user-friendly website and mobile app, ordering and tracking your delivery is a breeze.

At IIITeatz, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and customer service. We use only the freshest ingredients and work closely with our canteen partners to ensure that every dish meets our high standards of excellence. And if you ever have any questions or concerns, our dedicated customer support team is here to help.

Thank you for choosing IIITeatz Food Delivery. We look forward to serving you soon!
